Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At WorkBase, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Business information: Business name, business code, email address
  • Employee data: Names, roles, pay rates, work schedules, shift times
  • Usage data: Check-in/out times, shift hours, leave records
  • Payment data: Processed by Stripe — we never store card details

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the WorkBase service
  • To send transactional emails (welcome, PIN reset, weekly summaries)
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on servers in the EU. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Manager PINs are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Brevo — for transactional email delivery
  • Render/Infrastructure providers — for hosting

5. Employee Data

As a business owner using WorkBase, you are the data controller for your employees' data. You are responsible for informing employees that their work data is being processed and obtaining any required consents under applicable law (e.g. GDPR).

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (available via CSV export in the app)
  • Object to processing

7. Cookies

WorkBase uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your session. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@workbase.app.
