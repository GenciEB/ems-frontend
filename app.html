Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

WorkBase is built for small business owners who just want things to work — no bloat, no complexity.

⏱
Check In & Out
Employees clock in with their PIN. See who's on shift in real time, with late tracking and overtime automatically calculated.
🗓
Shift Scheduling
Plan the week ahead with a beautiful grid view. See scheduled vs actual, spot missed shifts instantly, edit with one tap.
💰
Payroll & Payslips
Automatic pay calculation based on hours worked. Generate professional payslips with deductions, tax and net pay.
🏖
Leave Management
Track holidays, sick days and personal leave per employee. See it all on the dashboard so you're never caught short.
📊
Live Dashboard
Real-time overview of who's in, hours worked this week, monthly payroll totals and attendance charts at a glance.
📱
Works on Any Device
Install WorkBase on your phone like a native app. Works offline too — no internet required once installed.
